About mmctl

"Give a man a fire and he's warm for a day, but set fire to him and he's warm for the rest of his life."

Terry Pratchett, Jingo

The first version of mmctl was written when I wanted to play a game with a friend and could not properly configure murmur fast enough. One hour later he had to leave and I had a new itch that needed scratching.

mmctl is available on GitHub. Feel free to send me feedback, suggestions, anything you can think of.

Other than numerous jQuery plugins, mmctl uses Bootstrap, which I think is the best thing Twitter has ever given me. The server-side uses Python, ZeroC's Ice and the irresistable Flask.
