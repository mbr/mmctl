About mmctl

"Give a man a fire and he's warm for a day, but set fire to him and he's warm for the rest of his life."

Terry Pratchett, Jingo

The first version of mmctl was written when I wanted to play a game with a friend and could not properly configure murmur fast enough. One hour later he had leave and I promptly started scratching the newfound itch.

mmctl is available on GitHub. Feel free to send me feedback, suggestions or anything else.

Other than numerous jQuery plugins, mmctl uses Bootstrap, which I think is the best thing Twitter has ever given me.
